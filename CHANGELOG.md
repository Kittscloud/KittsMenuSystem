- Added `Keybinds` everywhere - Keybinds are always added at the bottom of all menus so they can be used everywhere .
- Updated Exmaples - Uses `BaseSetting` and updated `Menu`.
- Updated `LICENSE` - License is now `AGPL v3.0`.
